# mk-piclock Kids BOM

## Build scope

This BOM is for one **mk-piclock kids** unit.

Assumptions:

- 1 Raspberry Pi Zero 2 WH per build
- 1 speaker per build
- 1 OLED display per build
- 1 RGB LED module per build
- 1 TP223B touch module per build
- 1 jumper wire set / ribbon cable per build
- 1 USB-C panel mount connector per build
- 4 rubber feet per build
- 1 MAX98357A I2S amplifier per build
- 1 USB-A to USB-C cable per build

## Not included

This BOM does **not** include:

- PLA or ASA filament
- Screws
- USB-A 5V 2A AC-to-DC adapter
- Solder, heat-shrink, ferrules, terminals, or other shop consumables
- SD card
- Shipping, taxes, duties, or marketplace fees

## Notes

- Rubber feet were calculated as a 16-piece pack with 4 feet used per build.
- Jumper wire cost was counted as one full purchase per build because no pack quantity was provided.
- MAX98357A amplifier cost was counted as one full purchase per build because no pack quantity was provided.
- Prices are based only on the values provided and were not checked against current supplier pricing.
